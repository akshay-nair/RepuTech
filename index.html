What is RepuTech?

We are a service following the sharing economy paradigm, a socio-economic system built around the sharing of human and physical resources.

In our case, users can offer their knowledge of various devices (PCs, laptops, Macs, smartphones) in order to help others in need regarding technological problems.

We enable people to help out others in their community.

How it works

Create a profile

Sign up with your information and get verified to begin.

Search for services

Whether you're looking for tech support or are offering your own time, use our search engine to discover an opportunity that suits you.

Use our built-in messaging program to arrange a meeting with another member.
